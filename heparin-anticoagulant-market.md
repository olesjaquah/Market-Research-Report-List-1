<p><p>The heparin anticoagulant market is segmented into three main types: Unfractionated Heparin (UF), Low Molecular Weight Heparin (LMWH), and Ultra-low Molecular Weight Heparin (ULMWH). UF heparin is a traditional form used for rapid anticoagulation and is often administered intravenously. LMWH offers more predictable pharmacokinetics and is favored for outpatient settings due to its ease of use and lower risk of complications. ULMWH provides even more refined dosing and reduced side effects, catering to specialized therapeutic needs.</p></p>
