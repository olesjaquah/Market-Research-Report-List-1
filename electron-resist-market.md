<p><strong>The Electron Resist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Electron Resist</li><li>Negative Electron Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron resist market is divided into two main types: positive electron resist and negative electron resist. Positive electron resists become more soluble when exposed to electron beams, allowing for precise patterning in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication. Conversely, negative electron resists become less soluble upon exposure, resulting in the remaining structures after development. Both types are crucial for advanced lithography applications, with specific advantages depending on the desired resolution and application in device fabrication and nanotechnology.</p></p>
