<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) systems are essential for promoting wound healing by applying sub-atmospheric pressure to the wound site. The traditional NPWT system typically consists of a multi-use pump connected to a dressing that provides continuous or intermittent suction. In contrast, single-use NPWT systems offer a portable, all-in-one solution, ideal for home care settings, eliminating the need for complex setup. Both types effectively reduce wound size, enhance blood flow, and minimize infection risk, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
