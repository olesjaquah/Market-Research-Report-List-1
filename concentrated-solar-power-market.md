<p><p>Concentrated Solar Power (CSP) technologies harness sunlight to generate electricity through various systems. Parabolic Trough Collectors use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, heating fluid for power generation. Solar Towers utilize a large array of mirrors called heliostats to direct sunlight to a central tower, where it generates steam to drive turbines. Other market types include Linear Fresnel, Dish Stirling systems, and hybrid solutions that combine CSP with conventional energy sources, enhancing efficiency and reliability in energy production.</p></p>
