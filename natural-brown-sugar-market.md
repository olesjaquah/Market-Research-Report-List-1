<p><strong>The Natural Brown Sugar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Light Brown Sugar</li><li>Dark Brown Sugar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural brown sugar market is primarily segmented into light brown sugar and dark brown sugar. Light brown sugar contains less molasses, resulting in a milder flavor and lighter color, making it suitable for baking and sweetening beverages. Dark brown sugar has a higher molasses content, providing a richer taste and deeper color, often used in robust recipes like gingerbread and barbecue sauces. Both types are valued for their natural caramel notes and are favored as healthier alternatives to refined white sugar.</p></p>
