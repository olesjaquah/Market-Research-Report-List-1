<p><p>The HVAC air ducts market consists of three main types: sheet metal ducts, flexible non-metallic ducts, and fiberglass ducts. Sheet metal ducts are durable and efficient, often used for longer runs and high-pressure systems. Flexible non-metallic ducts are lightweight and easy to install, making them suitable for tight spaces. Fiberglass ducts provide excellent thermal insulation and sound absorption, ideal for reducing energy costs and noise in commercial applications. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various installation needs and preferences.</p></p>
