<p><strong>The Surgical 3D Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithography, SLA (Liquid Based)</li><li>Fused Deposition Modeling, FDM (Solid Based)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering, SLS ( Powder Based)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical 3D Printing market includes several types of technologies. Stereolithography (SLA) utilizes liquid resins cured by ultraviolet light for high precision. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) employs thermoplastic filaments to create solid objects layer by layer. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses powdered materials, selectively fused by a laser, allowing for complex geometries and strong structures. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to various surgical applications, from creating models for pre-operative planning to producing custom implants and devices.</p></p>
