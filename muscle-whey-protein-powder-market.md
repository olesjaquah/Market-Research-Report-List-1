<p><p>The muscle whey protein powder market consists of three main types: concentrated whey protein, isolated whey protein, and hydrolyzed whey protein. Concentrated whey protein contains about 70-80% protein and retains beneficial nutrients, making it a popular choice for those seeking cost-effectiveness. Isolated whey protein is purer, with 90% or more protein, ideal for those needing low carbohydrates and fats. Hydrolyzed whey protein undergoes partial hydrolysis for faster absorption, often favored by athletes for quick recovery. Each type caters to specific dietary needs and fitness goals.</p></p>
