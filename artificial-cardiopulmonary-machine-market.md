<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump HLM</li><li>Double Roller Pump HLM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial cardiopulmonary machine market is categorized into single roller pump heart-lung machines (HLM) and double roller pump HLMs. Single roller pump HLMs utilize one roller pump to manage blood flow, offering a simpler design and lower operational complexity, making them suitable for less complex procedures. In contrast, double roller pump HLMs feature two roller pumps, enhancing flow control and efficiency, which is advantageous for more demanding surgeries, providing greater reliability and performance in maintaining perfusion during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
