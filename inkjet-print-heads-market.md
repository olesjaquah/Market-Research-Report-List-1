<p><strong>The Inkjet Print Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type (Bulk Piezo, Thin Film Piezo)</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet print heads market comprises two main types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric print heads utilize mechanical pressure to expel ink droplets, with subcategories including bulk piezo, which uses larger piezoelectric elements, and thin film piezo, employing more compact and precise designs for enhanced control. In contrast, thermal print heads operate by rapidly heating ink to create vapor bubbles, forcing droplets out. Each technology offers distinct advantages, impacting applications across commercial and industrial sectors, influencing print quality and speed.</p></p>
