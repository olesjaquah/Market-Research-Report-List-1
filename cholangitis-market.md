<p><p>The cholangitis market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Acute Cholangitis** is an infection of the bile duct, often caused by obstruction, leading to severe symptoms and requiring urgent treatment. </p><p>2. **Primary Sclerosing Cholangitis** involves progressive inflammation and scarring of bile ducts, commonly associated with liver diseases, resulting in significant complications.</p><p>3. **Secondary Sclerosing Cholangitis** arises from external factors like bile duct injury or obstruction, causing similar inflammation and damage.</p><p>4. **Recurrent Pyogenic Cholangitis** is characterized by repeated infections of the bile duct, often linked to specific underlying conditions. Each type presents unique challenges and treatment needs in the healthcare market.</p></p>
