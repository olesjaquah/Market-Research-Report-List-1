<p><strong>The Video Laryngoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channelled Video Laryngoscopy</li><li>Unchannelled Video Laryngoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Video Laryngoscopy market is categorized into two main types: channelled and unchannelled video laryngoscopy. Channelled video laryngoscopy features a specific channel for guiding an endotracheal tube, enabling easier intubation, particularly in difficult airway scenarios. In contrast, unchannelled video laryngoscopy lacks this guiding channel, offering a clearer view of the airway but requiring more skill for intubation. Both types are essential in enhancing visualization during procedures, improving patient outcomes, and reducing complications in airway management.</p></p>
