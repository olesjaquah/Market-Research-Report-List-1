<p><p>Nanofiltration systems are essential for water treatment and separation processes, offering two primary membrane types: spiral and tubular. Spiral membranes consist of flat sheets wound into a cylindrical configuration, providing a compact design and high surface area, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Tubular membranes, on the other hand, feature a larger diameter and a straight design, allowing for easier cleaning and maintenance, ideal for viscous or particulate-laden inputs. Both designs cater to specific operational requirements in the market.</p></p>
