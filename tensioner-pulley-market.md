<p><p>The tensioner pulley market is divided into two main types: manual tensioners and automatic tensioners. Manual tensioners require manual adjustment to maintain the correct belt tension, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for certain applications. In contrast, automatic tensioners adjust tension dynamically, using springs or hydraulic mechanisms, ensuring optimal performance and reducing maintenance needs. The choice between these types depends on factors such as vehicle design, performance requirements, and user preference, impacting overall market demand and growth.</p></p>
