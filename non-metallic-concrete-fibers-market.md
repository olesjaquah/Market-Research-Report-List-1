<p><p>The non-metallic concrete fibers market primarily consists of macro-synthetic and micro-synthetic fibers. Macro-synthetic fibers are larger in diameter and are used to enhance the structural integrity and durability of concrete, often improving resistance to cracking and fatigue. Micro-synthetic fibers, on the other hand, are finer and are incorporated to control plastic shrinkage cracking and enhance the overall performance of fresh and hardened concrete. Both types cater to diverse construction applications, improving the longevity and resilience of concrete structures.</p></p>
