<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel motor market features two main types: inner-rotor motors and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, typically resulting in better torque density and compact design, making them ideal for electric vehicles. In contrast, external-rotor motors have the rotor outside the stator, which provides a larger surface area for heat dissipation and higher efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing performance and energy efficiency in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
