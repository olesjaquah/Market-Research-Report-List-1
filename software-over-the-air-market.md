<p><p>Software Over The Air (SOTA) market can be divided into two primary types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. The Cellular market utilizes mobile networks to deliver software updates and services directly to devices, ensuring coverage in remote areas and enabling updates on-the-go. In contrast, the Wi-Fi market leverages local wireless networks, offering faster data transfer rates and reduced costs for large updates. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing device functionality and user experience while ensuring efficient management of software resources.</p></p>
