<p><p>The commercial insulation market encompasses various types, including wraps, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Wraps are flexible materials that encase pipes and ducts to enhance thermal performance and protect against moisture. Coatings provide a protective layer that improves insulation efficiency and durability. Adhesives play a crucial role in bonding insulation materials securely, while sealants fill gaps and joints, preventing air leakage and moisture infiltration. Together, these components contribute to energy efficiency, comfort, and cost savings in commercial buildings.</p></p>
