<p><p>Ventilated FIBC bags, designed to enhance airflow, come in two primary types: two-sided ventilated and four-sided ventilated. Two-sided ventilated bags feature mesh panels on opposite sides, allowing for partial air circulation, ideal for products sensitive to moisture and temperature. In contrast, four-sided ventilated bags incorporate mesh panels on all sides, providing maximum airflow and heat dissipation, suitable for bulk materials like grains or fertilizers. Both types aim to preserve product quality during storage and transport.</p></p>
