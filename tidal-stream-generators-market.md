<p><p>Tidal stream generators harness kinetic energy from moving water. Axial turbines operate like underwater wind turbines, aligned with water flow for optimal efficiency. Crossflow turbines capture energy from currents moving at various angles, suitable for diverse tidal conditions. Flow augmented turbines enhance flow through design features, boosting performance even in lower flow areas. Other types may include innovative designs or hybrid systems, catering to specific site conditions and energy requirements. Together, these technologies drive the tidal energy market forward.</p></p>
